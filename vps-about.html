Vancouver Pet Sitting

JQuery, Bootstrap 3, PHP, HTML5, CSS3, Responsive

The Vancouver Pet Sitting website viewed in large and small layouts

View Website

This is the homepage for Vancouver Pet Sitters, a professional, experienced and very caring pet sitting agency dedicated to providing exceptional care for clients' pets and homes throughout the Vancouver area. When they asked me to build their website, their main concern was a site that looks better and is more modern than the competiton, but they were also concerned with speed usability (on all devices), a quick delivery and an affordable price.

I obliged by building a fully responsive site that is simple in design, but looks and functions like any modern site would. I created the logo and edited the images using Photoshop, developed the site using Bootstrap 3, Html5 and CSS3. I've added some effects and a picture gallery with JQuery, and used PHP to process and send out the contents of the form on the contact page.

I'm very happy with the site Janos built for us! Thank you for a job well done.

Helen, Vancouver Pet Sitting
