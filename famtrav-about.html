Family Adventure Magazine

WordPress, SQL, PHP, HTML5, CSS3, Responsive, Javascript

The Family Adventure Magazine website viewed in large and small layouts

View Website

This is the homepage for Family Adventure Magazine, a one-of-a-kind information resource on family adventure travel. The website lists over 500 adventure providers around the world with contact information. Adventure seekers can access the trip of their dreams through the database, either by activity or geographical location or both.

I was a front end developer on a larger team that built the site. It is a WordPress site, and my main role involved fitting a WordPress theme we purchased to purpose. On top of that, because the data came from a pre-existing, static website, we all worked as a team to organize and insert it all into the WordPress database.

We often hire out development work, and it can be a frustrating process, because of the constant monitoring and adjustment required to achieve the proper results. Working with János has been a refreshing change. He did a great job designing and coding on the front end, his invaluable suggestions and improvements helped create a stable site, and his willingness to put in the required overtime helped complete the project on time.

Nick Vlacic, IDOLON.US
